On Set Services

DIT

The dawn of the digital age brought a major shift in the film industry. The transition from film to digital data and complex computer systems required a technician to safely and securely handle the film data. Our Digital Image Technician supports the seamless transition of assets from set to post-production - ensuring quality and security at all times. Our experienced team harnesses the latest technology and implements robust security protocols to ensure a safe, secure and speedy transfer of data on-set.

Location Sound

Our sound team provides scalable audio recording packages for all project types and on-site audio requirements. To ensure high quality audio output, we have invested in industry-leading equipment to provide pristine quality audio with a variety of accessories to ensure all metadata and timecode operations are in sync with the project requirements. Coupled with a meticulous attention to detail and passion for audio we ensure our recordings capture quality sound that surpass anything found in a SFX library.

Live Streaming

Live streaming allows you to interact directly with your target audience by streaming, recording and broadcasting content in real-time, providing network quality broadcast on live social media platforms around the world. Our streaming solutions provide real-time feeds from any video and audio source to virtually any platform - seamlessly connecting your brand with audiences. We can scale from low-resolution guide videos to full 4K broadcast solutions with remote internet connections and equipment anywhere the project requires.

On-set editing

On-set editing allows the Director and Creative teams to quickly assess creative decisions on location, saving time and allowing for full creative dexterity of ideas. Our on-set editing team are seasoned at working quickly and reacting decisively to expedite the creative process.

Photography

One of the most powerful tools a photographers has is the ability to communicate emotion and story through a lens. Our photography team specialise in elevating a brand’s image through visual storytelling using crafted images. Our team works intimately with the subject matter to ascertain how to bring out the brand’s mission, vision and emotion in its truest and most impactful sense. Whether it’s event coverage, news coverage, branding packages, or product campaigns, our creative team deliver images that convey emotion and visual elegance shot after shot

Videography

Capturing a moving image freezes a moment in time - our videography team are storytellers first, videographers second - making sure the moments we freeze tell a compelling story. For event coverage, corporate image films, commercials, documentaries, broadcast, and feature films, our team of videographers and directors deliver compelling stories crafted to resonate with audiences. Our packages scale to the requirements of any project, ensuring all elements are executed to the highest standards with a focus on unbeatable production quality for any budget.

Post Production

Editing

The editing process is where a story comes to life, taking raw data and crafting it to create a clear, creative and concise story. Starting with thousands of puzzle pieces, and ending with a captivating film is an art form in itself our team of editors work side-by-side our clients to craft a film’s narrative to concisely tell a brand’s message, story, and emotion through dynamic stylistic approaches for any type of media project. From explainer white-board videos, training seminars, corporate image films, documentaries or narrative works - we deliver compelling content crafted through passion.

Colour Grading

Colour grading has the power to elevate any film, bringing in richness and vibrance to allow the film’s craft to shine through. Our colour grading services take a crafted edit and injects it with an uplifting level of visual continuity and depth. Done well, colour grading is a delicate art form, subtly guiding the viewer through the story - adding tension, suspense or humour without viewers ever noticing. Our studios provide the latest & 4K HDR Dolby Vision standards catering to any type of deliverable and target medium.

Sound Design

Our award-winning audio team delivers a range of services to enhance any project. Our Foley, Sound Design, & ADR recording (Dubbing), combined with our mixing and mastering services come together to offer clients a sound environment to enhance all media types. Our crafted sound design is mixed to perfectly complement and enhance visual projects, bringing them to life to create an even more captivating narrative.Well crafted sound design is about more than just adding audio to visuals, it’s about adding realism to the improbable. Our main stage is equipped to handle Dolby Atmos for Television & Home Cinema, providing the highest attainable quality for any soundscape possible.

Motion Graphics

Adding another layer of creative expression to our films, Motion Graphics allows us to convey crucial information effectively while keeping the audience engaged. Our in-house team provides crafted 2D and 3D motion graphics which subtly present contact information or narrative-adding copy dynamically to support the retention of information without compromising creative integrity.



VFX

VFX has the ability to take any project to another level. From clean-ups, make-up beauty passes, set-extensions, CGI elements, compositing, full rotoscoping and animations - VFX delivers an image that tricks our eyes and brains into believing the reality our creativity constructs. Our in-house team delivers worldwide to industry-leading international productions, home-grown regional content creators and everyone in-between.
